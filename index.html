Moss Removal

Rooftop moss can shorten the life of your shingles and leave your home vulnerable to moisture damage. I take care of the problem safely and effectively by first removing the bulk moss by hand using a gentle nylon brush. Then, I apply an eco-friendly sodium percarbonate treatment to kill off remaining spores and prevent regrowth.

This method is tough on moss, but easy on your roof and the environment—no harsh chemicals, just a clean, protected roof ready to face the elements.

  • Debris Removal
  • Bulk Moss Removal
  • Final Soft Wash Treatment
